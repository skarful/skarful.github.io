Master Thesis (Jan 2018 - Sept 2018)
From Motions to Emotions: Classification of Full-body Movements using Deep Learning [paper 1] [paper 2] [code]
  • Conducted under the framework of the EU Horizon 2020 project WhoLoDancE at the InfoMus Lab, Genoa.
  • Created and processed a motion-capture dance dataset with four classes of emotions.
